About Us

Lingotran Private Limited is a digital language learning company based in Mysore, India. At Lingotran we are building a digital platform to teach foreign languages in a way that rapidly accelerates conversational ability.

  • One of our founders, Alexis, was born in Mysore, India, but grew up in London. Over the years he reached conversational ability in German, French, Portuguese, Spanish and Hindi.
  • We Want People To Make Stronger Connections Through Language
  • We Want To Build The Most Effective Language Learning Platform That Can Make Learners Become Conversationally Proficient In Two Months

Our Approach

By bridging the gap between traditional rigorous grammar-oriented teaching and technology-enabled spaced repetition, our platform significantly accelerates learning. The content is very contextual, which helps the learner stay engaged and motivated.
